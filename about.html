Pursuing a Passion

Artist

The dream started 10 years ago when the creator, Meg, attended her first artist alley at an anime convention in Southern California. She had no formal art education and pursued STEM in college. After entering the working world, she realized science wasn't her true passion. 2020 came along and the pandemic created newfound free time to pursue other things. She quickly became intrigued by other small artists making a living from selling their art and remembered how that had always inspired her. After working as a Lab Technician for 4 years, she finally found the means to pursue Stuck on Sketches as a full time artist and business owner in 2023.

Future Ambitions

Although the business is still small, SOS is slowly expanding its product line and introducing more fandoms and new content to appeal to a wide range of people! We want to have bits and pieces of everything we love available in our product line and aim to be a recognizable brand at bigger events in the future.
