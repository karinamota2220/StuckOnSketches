Stuck on Sketches (SOS)

Is a kawaii food and fanart inspired small business. We create many cute themed items like stickers, prints, and charms using our original art. SOS is currently operating in sunny San Diego California and attends many local and LA conventions/cupsleeves.

Pursuing a Passion

The dream started 10 years ago when the creator, Meg, attended her first artist alley at an anime convention in Southern California. She had no formal art education and pursued STEM in college. After entering the working world, she realized science wasn't her true passion. 2020 came along and the pandemic created newfound free time to pursue other things. She quickly became intrigued by other small artists making a living from selling their art and remembered how that had always inspired her. After working as a Lab Technician for 4 years, she finally found the means to pursue Stuck on Sketches as a full time artist and business owner in 2023.

Fun Facts

The artist and owner, Meg, is a boba addict and a huge foodie. She loves to stay up-to-date on all the recent anime/shows and also enjoys listening to DnD campaigns and true crime podcasts.

She is also a novice cosplayer that is obsessed with conventions and making new cosplayer/artist friends. One day she hopes to have a Shiba Inu coworker despite knowing about their stubborn and sassy nature.

Kai is the extroverted unofficial Sr. manager of goofiness that attends most events and 3D prints random displays for the booth set-up. He has no idea what most of the art is but does his best to find the stickers people ask for at events. He is very kind and reliable and has supported SOS even before it became Meg's full time gig.
